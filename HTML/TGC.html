Origin



The Gratitude Challenge was created by Seth Barnes, the founder of Adventures In Missions, in order to bring a sense of one-ness to those who have been affected by the recent outbreak of COVID-19. The original idea was presented to the public in his blog
" 10 Call Gratitude Challenge"

Why

We have started The Gratitude Challenge as a way to spread the message of being an action. We want to partner with those who are doing all they can to fight the virus and to spread love of Christ faster than the virus itself.
